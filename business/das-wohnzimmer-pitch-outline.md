## Presentation Tips

**Preparation:**
- Rehearse 3+ times (aim for 7-10 minutes)
- Time yourself (each slide ~1 minute)
- Prepare for questions (see objection handling)
- Charge your iPhone (demo device)
- Bring backup (iPad or laptop with slides)

**During Presentation:**
- Stand up (if in-person)
- Make eye contact
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Pause after key numbers (let them sink in)
- Ask questions ("Does this resonate?")
- Listen more than you talk

**After Presentation:**
- Don't push for immediate decision
- Offer to send proposal in writing
- Schedule follow-up (3-5 days)
- Thank them for their time
- Leave one-pager + business card

**If They Say "Yes":**
- Express excitement (but stay professional)
- Get menu data immediately (while momentum is high)
- Schedule staff training (next 7 days)
- Send pilot agreement (same day)

**If They Say "Let Me Think About It":**
- "Absolutely, what information can I provide to help with that decision?"
- "Would it help if I sent a detailed PDF?"
- "When would be a good time to follow up?"

**If They Say "No":**
- "I appreciate your time. Can I ask what your main concern is?"
- Document objection (refine pitch for next venue)
- "If anything changes, I'd love to work with you in the future."
