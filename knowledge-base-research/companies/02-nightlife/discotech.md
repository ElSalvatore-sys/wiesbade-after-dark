## Executive Summary

Discotech is a comprehensive nightlife management and booking platform that positions itself as "OpenTable for the $27 billion U.S. nightlife market." Founded in 2013 and headquartered in Los Angeles, the platform connects users with 1,400+ nightlife venues across 60+ cities globally, offering table reservations, guestlist signup, and ticket purchases through a unified mobile-first experience.

**Key Metrics:**
- 1M+ app downloads
- 4.9/5 star rating (iOS & Android)
- 1,400+ venue partners
- 60+ cities globally
- $7M gross revenue (2019)
- $875K net revenue (2019)
- 29 employees

## 2. Core Features & Services

### 2.1 Guest List Management
**User Features:**
- Free and discounted guestlist signup
- One-tap signup process
- Mobile phone verification at venue entry
- Ability to add up to +5 guests per signup
- Real-time guestlist status (open/closed)
- 24/7 customer support via call or text

**Venue Features:**
- Ability to pause/close guestlists at capacity
- Customer count tracking
- Foot traffic generation
- Commission-based revenue: $3-$5 per checked-in guest

**Process:**
1. User browses events and venues
2. Signs up for guestlist via one-tap
3. Receives confirmation
4. Venue verifies via mobile at entry
5. Discotech receives commission for checked-in guests

### 2.2 VIP Table & Bottle Service Reservations
**User Benefits:**
- Guaranteed venue admission (no door rejection risk)
- Shorter wait times vs. general admission
- Private table space for socializing
- VIP admissions included with table booking
- No cover charge or ticket required
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
- View floor plans and bottle menus
- One-tap booking and cancellation
- Rebook anytime flexibility
- Dedicated customer support

**Pricing Transparency:**
- Updated real-time table pricing
- Interactive floor plans
- Full bottle menus visible
- 25-33% savings at select venues (e.g., Encore Beach Club)
- Prices often lower than direct booking

**Commission Structure:**
- Venues pay 10-20% commission on gross customer spend
- Commission varies by venue partnership agreement
- No upfront fees for venues

### 2.3 Event Tickets
**Features:**
- Presale ticket access
- Digital tickets (no printing required)
- In-app purchase and management
- Face value + service fee pricing model

**Revenue Model:**
- Tickets sold at face value + service fee
- Face value returned to venue partners
- Service fee retained as Discotech commission

### 2.4 Event Discovery
**Capabilities:**
- Browse all events at all clubs in user's city
- Filter by venue type (clubs, lounges, pool parties, festivals)
- Filter by music genre
- Track favorite DJs and artists
- View venue photos and information
- See upcoming lineups and performers
- Real-time friend location visibility (social feature)

## 6. Business Model & Revenue Streams

### Revenue Breakdown (3 Primary Channels)

**1. VIP Table/Bottle Service (Largest Revenue Stream)**
- Commission: 10-20% of gross customer spend
- Variable based on venue partnership
- Example: $1,000 table spend = $100-$200 commission to Discotech

**2. Ticket Sales**
- Model: Face value + service fee
- Face value returned to venue
- Service fee retained as revenue
- Platform handles transaction directly

**3. Guest List Commission**
- Fee: $3-$5 per checked-in guest
- Paid by venue for foot traffic generation
- Only charged for guests who actually show up

### Financial Performance
- **Gross Revenue (2019):** ~$7M
- **Net Revenue (2019):** ~$875K
- **Net Margin:** 10-15% of gross sales
- **Monthly Gross Sales:** $1M+ (current)
- **Revenue Growth:** Year-over-year growth with minimal advertising spend

### Competitive Advantages
- No signup fees or subscription costs for users
- Transparent, predictable pricing
- Commission-only model aligns incentives with venues
- Lower customer acquisition cost vs. traditional promoters
- Scalable platform (not limited by promoter availability)

## 12. Key Takeaways

### What Discotech Does Exceptionally Well
1. **Unified platform** combining all nightlife booking needs
2. **Transparent pricing** with no hidden fees
3. **Commission-only model** aligned with venue success
4. **Mobile-first experience** with one-tap booking
5. **High user satisfaction** (4.9/5 rating maintained)
6. **Scalable model** (1,400+ venues, 60+ cities)
7. **Disrupted traditional promoter model** successfully
8. **Strong venue partnerships** with top-tier locations

### Gaps WiesbadenAfterDark Can Fill
1. **German/European market** underserved by Discotech
2. **Local cultural integration** (German nightlife culture differs from U.S.)
3. **Broader event scope** (cultural events, not just clubs)
4. **Enhanced social features** (community building)
5. **Safety and responsibility** features (important in German market)
6. **Public transportation integration** (critical in Europe)
7. **Loyalty programs** (not emphasized by Discotech)

- Transparent communication

### Risks to Avoid
- Don't rely solely on third-party promoters (like old model)

- Don't neglect mobile app quality
- Prevent lineup change communication gaps
- Maintain pricing consistency
- Don't over-complicate the booking process
