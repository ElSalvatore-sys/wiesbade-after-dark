# Resy - Premium Restaurant Booking Platform

## Company Overview

### Basic Information
- **Founded**: 2014
- **Founders**: Ben Leventhal (co-founder of Eater.com), Michael Montero (co-founder of CrowdTwist), Gary Vaynerchuk
- **Headquarters**: New York, USA
- **Acquisition**: Acquired by American Express in August 2019
- **Current Leadership**:
  - CEO: Ben Leventhal (co-founder, continued post-acquisition)
  - CTO: Michael Montero (co-founder, continued post-acquisition)
  - COO: Junaid Shams
  - CMO: Hannah Kelly

### Market Position
- **Restaurant Network**: Over 20,000 restaurants globally (as of 2025), up from 16,000 in 2023 and 4,000 at time of acquisition
- **Geographic Coverage**: 154+ U.S. cities and 10+ countries
- **Weekly Diners**: 2.6+ million diners per week
- **Market Share**: 12.47% in Restaurant Reservation Software category
- **Customer Base**: 8,442 restaurant customers
- **Focus Markets**: Strong presence in major urban centers - NYC, Los Angeles, London, Sydney

### Strategic Positioning
Resy occupies the premium/luxury segment of restaurant reservations, focusing on high-end dining experiences, trendy hotspots, natural wine bars, and tasting menus. The platform emphasizes quality over quantity, maintaining selective market presence rather than aggressive global expansion.

## Core Features - Consumer Platform

### 1. Reservation Management

#### Standard Booking
- Real-time availability calendar view
- Instant booking confirmation
- Party size selection (typically 1-20+ guests)
- Time slot selection with visual availability indicators
- Dietary restrictions and special occasion notes
- Preferred seating selection (chef's counter, window, corner, etc.)

#### Notify Feature (Signature Feature)
- **Purpose**: Future waitlist system that alerts users when tables become available
- **How it Works**: Users set preferences (date, time range, party size) and receive real-time alerts via push notifications and email when tables open up
- **Performance Data**: Sunday is the best day for Notify conversions
- **User Responsibility**: Receiving a Notify alert is NOT a guaranteed reservation - users must claim the table by clicking the alert
- **10-Year Anniversary**: Launched in 2015, still a core differentiator

#### Hit Lists
- Curated restaurant collections
- Personalized recommendations
- "Climbing" - trending restaurants
- "New On Resy" - recently added venues
- Editorial collections: "Cozy Locales," "Splurge Worthy," "Don't Miss Dessert"
- Geo-targeted by city

### 2. Mobile Apps

#### iOS App Features
- Full reservation management
- Push notifications for Notify alerts
- Discover Tab with original editorial content
- Map view with availability filters
- Invite guests functionality
- Hit Lists and Collections
- American Express Global Dining Access integration

#### Android App (Relaunched May 2025)
- **Availability**: Google Play Store, Android 12.0+
- **Current Features**: Notify, Hit Lists, Invite Guests, exclusive experiences, Global Dining Access
- **Coming Soon**: Discover Tab, enhanced map filters, editorial content integration
- **Significance**: Connects millions of Android users to 20,000+ restaurants after platform relaunch

### 3. Premium Access Programs

#### Global Dining Access (American Express)
- **Launch**: 2021
- **Eligible Cards**: American Express Platinum and select premium cards
- **Benefits**:
  - Exclusive reservations at 650+ in-demand restaurants
  - Priority Notify status
  - Last-minute table options
  - Nightly tables at premium venues
  - Early access to Resy events

#### Platinum Nights by Resy
- **Exclusive Access**: Dedicated reservation nights at coveted restaurants
- **Locations**: New York, Los Angeles, Miami (expanding in 2026)
- **Access**: resy.com/platinum and Resy iOS app
- **Target**: American Express Platinum Card Members

### 4. Social & Sharing Features

#### Guest Management
- Invite guests to reservations via app
- Share dining plans with friends

#### Cultural Features

## Pricing Structure

### Consumer Side
- **Free to Browse**: No charge for searching restaurants
- **Reservation Fees**: Premium fee model for high-demand tables
  - Range: $10-$50 per reservation
  - Split between Resy and restaurant partners
  - Only charged for select premium slots

### Restaurant Side (ResyOS)

#### Subscription Tiers
1. **Basic Tier**
   - Price: $249/month
   - Restricted features
   - No per-cover fees on online reservations

2. **Pro Tier** (Most Popular)
   - Price: $399/month
   - Full feature set
   - No per-cover fees

3. **Enterprise Tier**
   - Price: $899/month
   - Advanced features
   - Dedicated support
   - No per-cover fees

#### Additional Costs
- **POS Integration**: +$100/month
- **Event Ticketing**: Up to 3% transaction fee
- **No Per-Cover Fees**: Major differentiator from OpenTable

#### 1. Discoverability
- Clean, uncluttered restaurant pages

- Large, central "Reserve Now" button
- Prominent search bar at expected location (top of screen)

#### 2. Calendar View
- Instant overview of availability
- Reduces user friction
- Industry-leading booking flow
- Visual date selection

#### 3. Trust Signals
- Vetted restaurant network reduces research burden
- Editorial curation builds confidence
- Social proof integration
- Restaurant photography and details

#### 4. Mobile-First Design
- App opens directly to Search page
- Large, discoverable search bar
- Touch-optimized interface
- Push notifications for time-sensitive alerts

#### 5. Personalization
- "Save my details" for returning guests
- Guest preference tracking
- Recommended for You algorithm
- Geo-targeted content

### Areas for Improvement (Learnings)

#### 1. Search Clarity
- **Issue**: "Search Current Location" label is misleading
- **Solution**: Change to simply "Search" to remove confusion
- **Learning**: Clear, concise labels over specific terminology

#### 2. Discover Integration
- **Issue**: Discover and search are separate, creating disconnect
- **Solution**: Integrate search into Discover or add filtering
- **Learning**: Users expect unified discovery experiences

#### 3. Android Parity
- **Issue**: Android app still catching up to iOS features
- **Solution**: Prioritize platform parity for multi-platform user base
- **Learning**: Don't alienate significant user segments

### Recommended UX Patterns

#### Booking Flow Animation
- Progress bars for multi-step bookings
- Celebratory confetti on confirmation
- Positive association building
- Increases conversion rates

#### Quick Pickers
- "Next available slot" shortcuts
- Time range selectors
- Party size presets
- Reduce decision fatigue

#### Confirmation Systems
- Immediate email confirmation
- SMS notifications
- Calendar export options
- Clear cancellation policies

## Best Practices - Restaurant Operations

### No-Show Prevention Strategy

#### 1. Automated Reminders
- SMS and email confirmations
- Pre-arrival reminders (24-48 hours)
- Day-of reminders (morning of reservation)
- **Result**: Contributes to 3.49% average no-show rate

#### 2. Payment Requirements
- **Pre-Pay Feature**: PCI-compliant text payment requests
- **Ticketing**: Upfront payment for set menus
- **Deposits**: Industry data shows 3 in 4 diners accept deposits
- **Effective Amounts**: Even £5-10 deposits dramatically improve attendance

#### 3. Notify Waitlist
- Fills cancelled reservations instantly
- Maximizes capacity utilization
- Keeps restaurants full
- "Best part of the software" according to many restaurants

#### 4. Guest History Tracking
- Flag repeat no-show offenders
- Require deposits for high-risk guests
- Risk-based policies vs. blanket rules
- AI-powered predictive flagging

#### 5. Grace Period Policies
- Standard: 10-15 minute grace period
- Release tables to waitlist after grace period
- Prevents empty table time waste
- Clear policy communication

### Revenue Optimization

#### 1. Table Optimization
- Automatic table combination algorithms
- Maximize dining room capacity
- Reduce empty seats
- Dynamic floor plan adjustments

#### 2. Service Pace Control
- Control covers per section/time
- Real-time availability adjustments
- Custom flow for special events
- Prevent kitchen/service overload

#### 3. Event Management
- Pre-paid ticketing reduces no-shows
- A la carte add-ons diversify revenue
- Private events for VIPs
- Merchandise and product sales

#### 4. VIP & Exclusive Access
- Allocate premium inventory for high-value guests
- American Express network access
- Build loyalty through exclusivity
- Higher spending guest segments

### Guest Relationship Management

#### 1. Data-Driven Personalization
- Track preferences and dining history
- Special occasion reminders
- Dietary restriction documentation
- Surprise and delight opportunities

#### 2. Automated Surveys
- Private post-dining feedback
- Guest insights for service refinement
- Maintain high satisfaction levels
- Continuous improvement loop

#### 3. Performance Benchmarking
- Compare with other restaurants in city
- Identify areas for improvement
- Track cover count trends
- Make informed business decisions
