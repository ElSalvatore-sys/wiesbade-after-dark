# Privacy Policy - WiesbadenAfterDark

**Last Updated:** December 22, 2025

## Overview

WiesbadenAfterDark ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Name (optional, for personalization)
- Phone number (optional, for reservations)

### Usage Data
- Venues you visit (check-ins)
- Events you attend
- Loyalty points earned
- Preferences and favorites

### Device Information
- Device type and OS version
- App version
- Push notification tokens

### Location Data (Optional)
- Only when you enable location services
- Used to show nearby venues
- Never shared with third parties

## How We Use Your Data

- **Authentication:** To verify your identity and secure your account
- **Personalization:** To show relevant venues and events
- **Loyalty Program:** To track and reward your visits
- **Notifications:** To send updates about events and offers (with your permission)
- **Analytics:** To improve the app experience (anonymized)

## Data Storage

- Data is stored securely on Supabase (EU servers)
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Local data is stored in encrypted SwiftData containers

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data
- Share data with advertisers
- Use third-party tracking

We may share data with:
- Venue partners (only your check-in status, with consent)
- Legal authorities (if required by law)

## Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and data
- **Export** your data
- **Withdraw** consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@ea-solutions.de

## Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users 17 years and older due to nightlife content (bars, clubs). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17.

## Data Retention

- Account data: Until you delete your account
- Check-in history: 2 years
- Analytics: 1 year (anonymized)

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
- Secure token-based authentication
- Regular security audits

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website.
