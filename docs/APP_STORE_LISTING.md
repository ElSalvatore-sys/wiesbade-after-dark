## Description (English)

**Discover Wiesbaden's Best Nightlife**

WiesbadenAfterDark is your ultimate guide to bars, clubs, and events in Wiesbaden, Germany.

**Features:**
- Discover top-rated venues near you
- Browse upcoming events and parties
- Check in and earn loyalty points
- Redeem rewards at your favorite spots
- Connect with the nightlife community
- Beautiful, intuitive dark-mode design

**Loyalty Program:**
Earn points with every visit. Climb the tiers from Bronze to Platinum and unlock exclusive rewards!

**For Venue Owners:**
Partner with us to reach Wiesbaden's nightlife enthusiasts. Contact us for our Owner Portal.

**Coming Soon:**
- Apple Wallet integration
- Table reservations
- Event reminders

Download now and never miss a night out again!
